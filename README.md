### Original problem statement

```
Problem statement word for word : Design a simple credit risk calculator

Objective:
Design and present a basic Credit Score Calculation System that evaluates an individual's credit risk based on key financial and personal inputs.

Background:
Financial institutions assess credit risk to determine how likely a person is to repay a loan. Your task is to simulate a simplified version of this process by building a system that takes in user data and outputs a basic credit risk score or category (e.g., Low, Medium, High).

Inputs to be Collected from the User:
Monthly Salary.

Current Savings.

Monthly Mortgage Payout.

Number of Financial Dependents.

Employment Status (e.g., Full-time, Part-time, Unemployed).

Monthly Credit Card Spending.

Other Monthly Loan Repayments (e.g., car loan, student loan).

Presentation of 4 Slides :
Your understanding of the problem statement.

Simple Credit Risk formula that you have used within your project.

Solution Design – Diagram with all the components involved and its interactions.

Mock up screen of your user interface – This can be as simple as in excel sheet or ms-paint.

Guided Steps – Mandatory:
Problem Statement Understanding: Depict your understanding of problem statement.

Basic Credit Risk Logic/Formula: Design a logic or formula that uses these inputs to calculate a credit risk score or risk category. Can use AI tool to derive the credit risk formula.

Solution Design: Draw a basic diagram on how the components are interacting with each other and its technology stack.

User Interface: Provide a sample user interface that you expect to show it to the users.

Bonus Step – Optional:
Provide a working prototype on the look and feel of your product.
```
